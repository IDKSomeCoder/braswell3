How to Purchase

We offer two different options for purchasing:

  • Pre-made
  • Custom

Pre-Made:


For our pre-made bracelets, you can choose from an assortment of bracelets ranging from all kinds of elequent designs and patterns. Browse through our inventory in Mrs. Braswell's room 635 for a better look at it.

Custom:


If you are looking for a more unique style, don't fret! We have a fully customizable department whom make bracelets to your exact ideal product. Just send us a rough-draft or come in-person to place an order for your custom bracelet!

Cost and Cause

Our high-quality hand-made bracelets come at a reasonable cost of $2 for pre-made bracelets to up to $3 for a custom model bracelet. If this seems still too high for you and you feel your wasting your money, don't worry! We are setting up 50% of all our profit to fund the American Red Cross Organization so you can feel good with what you are buying! With your help, we can make the world a better place while making it stylish.
